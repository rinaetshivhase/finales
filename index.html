About us

Our History

MAMBURU ENGINEERING AND TRANSPORTATION SERVICES (Pty) Ltd Reg No.2024/237290/07 was founded in 2020 and is a family-type business. The Company was moved to the current premises in 2024. In 2023, MAMBURU ENGINEERING AND TRANSPORTATION SERVICES (Pty) Ltd works was purchased and Incorporated into the Ngudza village operation

Company Mission

At MAMBURU ENGINEERING AND TRANSPORTATION SERVICES (Pty) Ltd, our mission is to transform paper waste into recycled products and provide reliable transportation. We focus on sustainability, trust-based relationships, and continuous improvement, while ensuring a safe, inclusive work environment.

Our Vision

At MAMBURU ENGINEERING AND TRANSPORTATION SERVICES (Pty) Ltd, we aim to lead in sustainable paper recycling and eco-friendly transportation. We focus on innovation, efficiency, lasting partnerships, and sustainability to minimize waste and support a circular economy

Produts

Papers

Boxes

Tissues

Our range of recycled paper products includes packaging materials such as boxes and bags, printing paper available in both white and colored varieties, tissue paper like toilet paper and paper towels, and cardboard tubes and cores. Additionally, we handle other recycled materials including plastic recyclables such as bottles and containers, glass recyclables like bottles and jars, and metal recyclables such as cans and foil.

Services

Processing and manufacturing

Collection and sorting

Waste collection and transportation

Our company offers comprehensive services in paper recycling, including collection, sorting, processing, and education. We provide transportation for waste and recycling materials, along with logistics management. Our waste management services cover assessment, reduction strategies, and composting. Additionally, we offer consulting in sustainability, recycling program development, and waste management planning.

Managing Director
